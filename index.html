    Introduction

    v3 Data Models and Business Applications for WINDEV, WEBDEV and WINDEV Mobile

     - v3 Data Models and Applications have been redesigned to be more simple to understand and use, without losing any of their main prior functionality - 


    The alpha360 v3 Business applications are core business applications - ERPs, Sales and Hospitality apps -  based on the alpha360 v3 Universal Data Models.

    They cannot be used as is and cannot be immediately distributed to your customers/or used internally - you must  first study them, extend them and re brand them, according to your country and/or your specific industry.


    In v3 there are 2 Business applications:

     - the ERP Business application - supports Products, Inventory, Parties, Sales and Revenues, Purchases and Expenses, Retail and GL Accounting - in the following 3 versions:

    • the Windows (or C/S) version - is a full ERP implementation,
    • the WEB version - is a Sales only implementation,
    • the Mobile version -  is a Sales only implementation,

    . 

    - the Hospitality application - supports Hotels, Restaurants, Bars and the TakeAway Industry - currently in a Windows version only.

    • the Windows (or C/S) version -is a full  implementation of the Hospitality functionality.


    The windows ERP and the WEB Sales application both come in Open Source and Commercial versions.

    All other Business applications are Commercial versions only.


    * Please note that Open Source does NOT mean free - you must read the terms of use and the FAQs to better understand the licenses - and of course agree with the license terms before using them.
